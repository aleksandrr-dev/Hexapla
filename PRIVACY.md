# Privacy Policy — Hexapla / Гексапла

_Last updated: 6 July 2026_

Hexapla is an offline Bible reading app. It is built to know nothing about you.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking SDKs, and no server of ours that it talks to. We never see your reading activity, notes, bookmarks, or anything else.

## What stays on your device

All app data is stored locally on your device only: settings, bookmarks, notes, highlights, reading-plan progress, reading streak, and your last reading position. If you use the backup feature, the backup file is created where you choose to save it and is never transmitted anywhere by the app. Uninstalling the app deletes its local data.

## Network access

The app works fully offline with one exception: **narrated audio**. If you enable "Narrated audio (KJV)" in Settings, the app downloads public-domain LibriVox recordings directly from archive.org (the Internet Archive) and caches them on your device for offline listening. These are ordinary file downloads; the app sends no personal information with them. Archive.org's own privacy policy applies to those requests, as with any web download: https://archive.org/about/terms.php

## Payments

Optional tips ("Support the developer") are processed entirely by Google Play Billing on Google Play builds, or open an external payment page (ЮMoney) in your browser on other builds. The app itself never sees or stores payment details. Tips unlock nothing.

## Permissions

- **Notifications** — for the optional daily reading reminder and the media controls shown during audio playback.
- **Internet** — only for downloading narration audio as described above.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, children included.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated text will be published at this same address with a new date.

# Политика конфиденциальности — Гексапла

Приложение Гексапла — офлайн-читалка Библии. Оно **не собирает никаких данных**: нет аккаунтов, аналитики, рекламы и трекеров. Все данные (настройки, закладки, заметки, выделения, прогресс планов чтения) хранятся только на вашем устройстве и удаляются вместе с приложением. Доступ в интернет используется единственно для загрузки озвучки (записи LibriVox с archive.org), если вы её включили. Пожертвования обрабатываются Google Play или внешней страницей ЮMoney — приложение не видит платёжных данных. Вопросы: [@Hexapladev](https://t.me/Hexapladev).
