DRAFT 2 — Vilnius University Chylinski Bible project
(chylinskibible.flf.vu.lt), for the 1657-1660 NT (litmus 7/7)
=====================================================================
Subject: Permission request — Chylinski NT text in a free Bible app

Dear colleagues,

I develop Hexapla, a free offline parallel-Bible app for Android (no
ads, no analytics, nothing paid;
https://aleksandrr-dev.github.io/Hexapla/) — 29 classical translations
in 24 languages, each credited in the app.

Your edition of Chylinski's New Testament is a remarkable resource, and
the only complete machine-readable early Lithuanian NT I have found.
Your site licenses it for scholarly and educational purposes; a free
evangelistic app sits outside that grant, so I am writing to ask
whether you would permit the plain verse text (without your editorial
apparatus) to be included in this free app, with the project and
Vilnius University credited in the app's sources section.

If permission is possible under conditions, I will follow them
gladly. Thank you for the care this edition so clearly embodies.

With respect,

Aleksandr Ratchkov
aleksratchkov@gmail.com
https://github.com/aleksandrr-dev
